Our Founder

The Human Fund was founded by George Costanza (aka Art Vandelay) as a way to get out of giving his co-workers Christmas gifts.

The Costanza's celebrated Festivus, and the giving of fake Human Fund donations to office workeres in lieu of real holiday gifts contines to this day!

Our Work

The Human Fund offers money for people. Everyone needs money, everyone is a person. Person is singular for people, so if you are a person, you should like the human fund.

More than just money (for people), the Human Fund offers a chance for those that celebrate non-traditional holiday events, and those who don't celebrate at all, a chance to show their differences with a bit of levity.
